Slidetech Charger GT Specs

Engine: 5.0L naturally aspirated V8
Horsepower: 460 hp
Torque: 420 lb-ft
Transmission Options: 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic
0-60 mph: Approximately 4.0 seconds (with 10-speed automatic)
Top Speed: Around 155 mph (electronically limited)
Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive (RWD)
Suspension: Independent rear suspension, with available MagneRide adaptive dampers
Brakes: Brembo front brakes included in the Performance Package for added stopping power
Exhaust: Available active-valve exhaust system with adjustable sound modes
Length: 188.3 inches
Width: 75.4 inches
Height: 54.3 inches
Weight: Around 3,700 lbs (varies based on options)
Wheels: Standard 18-inch, with options for 19-inch wheels in various finishes
Infotainment: Standard 4.2-inch screen, with available SYNC 3 system (8-inch touchscreen) with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
Sound System: Available 9-speaker audio system or 12-speaker B&O premium sound system
Driver Assistance: Available adaptive cruise control, blind-spot monitoring, and pre-collision assist
