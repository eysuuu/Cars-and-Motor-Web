OUR HISTORY

Slide Tech, founded in 1977 by race driver Andres Ramos, began by selling premium vehicle parts from Germany and Japan. In 1989, the company introduced the Aurelio, a car that highlighted Philippine automotive innovation. Over the years, Slide Tech expanded its offerings, including a 400cc motorcycle, gaining a reputation for quality and innovation. In 2023, new business partners joined to support continued growth. Today, Slide Tech remains committed to delivering high-tech, reliable, and high-quality products while upholding its founder’s values.

OUR MISSION

To be one of the most reliable and sustainable car and motor companies in the world, providing the highest quality production and safe performance for customer satisfaction, Slide Tech strives to enhance and sustain a world-class experience through its environmental innovations.

OUR VISION

Slide Tech aims to be a global transportation company that evolves with new technology and delivers high-quality innovations for the future, making transportation more accessible and efficient for people around the world.
